Dhoni captaine

Mahendra Singh Dhoni (MS Dhoni) is a former captain of the Indian cricket team and one of the most successful cricketers in Indian history: Captaincy Dhoni is the only captain in cricket history to win all three major ICC trophies: the World Cup (2011), the Champions Trophy (2013), and the World Twenty20 (2007). He also holds the record for most matches as captain in Tests+ODIs+T20Is. Leadership Dhoni is known for his unorthodox captaincy, approachability, and cool-headed demeanor on the field, which has earned him the nickname "Captain Cool". IPL Dhoni has been the captain of the Chennai Super Kings (CSK) since the first edition of the Indian Premier League (IPL) in 2008. He led the team to two consecutive titles in 2010 and 2011. Other accomplishments Dhoni was selected for the 1997/98 Vinoo Mankad Trophy Under-16 Championship. He also represented Bihar and then the Jharkhand Cricket team in domestic cricket. Awards Dhoni received the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna in 2007-08 and LG's People's Choice Award in 2013.
